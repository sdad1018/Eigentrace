# EigenTrace
**Measuring How Language Models Reshape What They Say Under Constraint**

Every day, EigenTrace sends the same breaking news to ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek, and Grok. Fifteen independent measurement layers detect not just what models avoid — but how they soften, generalize, and hedge the language they do produce.

Three phenomena, measured mathematically:
- **High-coherence / low-consensus clusters** — ideas that hang together but models collectively avoid
- **Language compression** — verbs softened (measured by zipf frequency drift), names erased, hedges inserted
- **Narrative pressure points** — high agreement + systematic term avoidance

The system doesn't filter noise — it measures it. Each void word is labeled HIGH_SALIENCE, EMBEDDING_SIGNAL, or GENERIC_ARTIFACT based on source presence and frequency across thousands of stories.
